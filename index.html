Hi, I'm Aryabhatta, and I'm considered the foremost among the great mathematician-astronomers from the classical era of Indian mathematics and astronomy.

My work in mathematics included advancements in number theory and algebra , while in astronomy, I made important contributions to the study of planetary motion and the calculation of celestial events such as an eclipses.

I am honoured to have a satellite named after me and to be remembered as a significant figure in the history of science and mathematics.
