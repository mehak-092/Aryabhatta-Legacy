Verse 6 (c-d)

  • Volume of Right Pyramids
  • The rule is based on speculation on the analogy of the area of a triangle, and is inaccurate.

  • The correct formula is found to occur in the Brahma-sphuta-siddhanta of Brahmagupta where it is stated as follows:

    • "The volume of a uniform excavation divided by three is the volume of the needle-shaped solid."


  • In simple terms,

    • Volume of a cone or pyramid= (area of base) x (height).
