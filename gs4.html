  • Step 2. subtr

  • On the analogy of varga and avarga classification of the letters, the notational places are also divided into the varga and avarga places.

    • 1. The odd places denoting the units’ place, the hundreds’ places, the ten thousands’ place and so on, are called the varga places (because 1, 100, 10,000 etc are perfect squares).

    2. The even places denoting the tens’ place, the thousands’ place and so on are called the avarga places (because 10, 1000 etc are non-square numbers).
